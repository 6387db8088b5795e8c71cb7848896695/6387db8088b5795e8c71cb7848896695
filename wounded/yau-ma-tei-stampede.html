油麻地人踩人 Yau Ma Tei Stampede

理大圍城翌日，即11月18日，大批示威者響應網上號召，於油尖旺一帶聲援理大示威者。

事發在當晚深夜，大批防暴警察於午夜前沿彌敦道向油麻地方向推進，多次發射催淚彈驅散一批示威者，幾十人爭相走避，現場傳出多響懷疑槍聲，多人受傷倒地，有人被踩斷手。

晚上10時53分，消防員接報前往港鐵油麻地站處理一宗二級火警自動報火警鐘事故。大約晚上11時30分，消防人員在該站A1出口外處理火警期間，發現有大批人士從彌敦道走向碧街方向。傳真社表示，於大約11時半在彌敦道近碧街曾出現至少8下疑似槍聲的巨響及閃光。

晚上11時32分，消防處接獲前線消防員報告指，油麻地港鐵站A1出口與寶寧大廈之間的通道有「人疊人」的情況出現，立刻向消防通訊中心報告及要求增援。消防通訊中心隨即陸續增派另外9輛消防車及41輛救護車到場處理。

警方事後將窩打老道、碧街一帶圍封，窩打老道及彌敦道泊滿救護車候命。在增援人員未到達前，在場消防員立即進行拯救行動，用了大約10分鐘將30多名被困者拯救到安全地方。事故中，消防處沒有收到消防人員被在場警員阻撓救援的報告。

閉路電視片段顯示，有警員驅趕急救員及記者離開。另一網上片段顯示，有警員不但未即時疏導人堆，反在人堆後方噴射胡椒噴劑。救護員及後到場及分流傷者，有一名男子被抬出時，腿部僵硬，經急救後才睜開眼睛，由救護車送院。事發時數十人疊起至5至6層，高度與一名成年男子相若，期間有被困人士面部發紫，警方速龍小隊其後趕至阻止他們施救。

據傳真社片段顯示，約100人已被制服在地上，大量消防員及救護員在場照顧傷者，當中有至少13名傷者已躺在擔架床，部分人狀甚痛苦。

凌晨1點07分，當值助理消防區長通報消防通訊中心，指現場大約有150名示威者被捕，暫未能確實傷者人數。

而根據消防處內部文件，現場救護事故主管在凌晨2時15分報告，稱現場共有16名紅色、9名黃色及41名綠色傷者，距離消防人員最早報告時間，超過兩個半小時。

至凌晨4點31分，最後一名需要送院治理的傷者被送往聯合醫院治理。事件中共33人需要送院。醫院管理局表示，在當晚各區公眾活動中，8間公立醫院合共接收45名傷者。(Wikipedia)

The next day of the Siege of Polytechnic University, on November 18, a large number of demonstrators responded to the online call to support PolyU demonstrators in the Yau Tsim Mong area. In the middle of the night, a large number of riot police advanced in the direction of Yau Ma Tei on the front of Nathan Road at midnight, firing tear gas several times to disperse a group of demonstrators. Dozens of people rushed to avoid it. Many suspected gunshots were heard at the scene, and many were injured and fell on the ground, someone's arm was trampled on and broke.

At 10:53 pm, firefighters received a report and went to the Yau Ma Tei MTR Station to handle a second-level automatic fire alarm. At about 11.30 pm, while firefighters were dealing with the fire outside Exit A1 of the station, a large number of people were found heading towards Pitt Street from Nathan Road. The Factware News Agency stated that at about 11:30 there had been at least eight loud sounds and flashes of suspected gunfire on Nathan Road near Pitt Street.

At 11:32 pm, the Fire Services Department received a report from the frontline firefighters that there was a "passage of people" in the passageway between Exit A1 of Yau Ma Tei MTR Station and Po Ning Building, and immediately reported to the Fire Communication Center and requested reinforcements. The fire communication center immediately dispatched another 9 fire trucks and 41 ambulances to the scene for processing. Police later sealed off Waterloo Road and Pitt Street. Waterloo Road and Nathan Road were filled with ambulances on call. Before the reinforcements arrived, the firefighters at the scene immediately carried out rescue operations and took about 10 minutes to rescue more than 30 trapped people to a safe place. During the accident, the Fire Services Department did not receive any reports that firefighters were blocked by police officers at the scene.

CCTV footage shows police officers driving rescuers and journalists away. Another online clip showed that some police officers did not immediately guide the crowd, but sprayed pepper spray behind the crowd. When a man was carried out by ambulancemen, his legs were stiff, and his eyes were opened only after emergency treatment. He was taken to the hospital by ambulance. Dozens of people folded up to the 5th to 6th levels at the time of the incident. The height was similar to that of an adult man. During the period, the face of some of the trapped people became purple. Police Special Force team rushed to the scene and prevented ambulancemen from rescue.

According to the Factware News Agency footage, about 100 people have been subdued to the ground. A large number of firefighters and ambulancemen were present at the scene to take care of the injured. At least 13 of them were lying on stretcher beds, and some of them were suffering. At 1:07 a.m., the assistant fire chief on duty informed the fire communication center that about 150 demonstrators were arrested at the scene, and the number of injured was temporarily unknown. According to the internal documents of the Fire Services Department, the ambulance accident supervisor at the scene reported at 2:15 in the morning that there were 16 red, 9 yellow and 41 green injuried people at the scene, more than two and a half hours before the earliest report by firefighters.

As of 4.31 am, the last injured person who needed to be sent to hospital was sent to United Hospital for treatment. A total of 33 people needed to be sent to the hospital. The Hospital Authority stated that eight public hospitals received a total of 45 wounded during public events in various districts that night.
