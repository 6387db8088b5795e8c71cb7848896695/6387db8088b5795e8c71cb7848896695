楊子俊老師 Teacher Yeung Chi Jun

2019年6月12日，在拔萃女書院任教通識科的楊子俊老師，在金鐘的反修例警民衝突中，被橡膠子彈或布袋彈槍擊右眼及胸口，右眼嚴重受傷，視網膜崩裂，黃斑區亦有大裂孔，視力嚴重受損，幾近失明。楊老師在醫院接受治療時被警方以「暴動罪」拘捕。楊老師年近30歳，為香港大學社會科學學士，持7年教師年資。

On June 12, 2019, during the anti-lab police and civilian conflicts in Admiralty, Young Chi Jun, a Liberal Studies teacher at the Diocesan Girls' College, was shot by a rubber bullet or a bean bag round on the right eye and chest, and his right eye was seriously injured. The retina is cracked, and there are large holes in the macula. His visual acuity was seriously impaired and he was almost blinded. Teacher Young was arrested by the police for "rioting" while receiving treatment at the hospital. Mr. Young is nearly 30 years old and holds a bachelor's degree in social sciences from the University of Hong Kong. He holds 7 years of teacher education.
