郭先生 Kwok Chi Wai, Calvin (25)

香港人為香港付出左幾多，得返黎又係幾多，出過聲，流過淚，受過傷，流過血，但係出黎既結果永永遠遠都唔係你所想！
我一路相信人定勝天，個天愈俾難關我，我愈要克服，我信我自己，
原來命就係命，改變唔到就係改變唔到，我今次輸左俾個天。
但係香港人未輸，邊個撐到最後邊個就會贏，香港人加油！
How much had the Hong Kong people given to Hong Kong, and how much have they gotten back? We've voiced out, shed tears, suffered from injuries, shed blood, but the results that came out were never what you wish! I always believe people's willpower will overcome destiny. The more hardship destiny gives me, the harder I overcome it. I believe in myself. It turns out destiny is destiny. When you can't change it you can't change it. I've lost to destiny this time. But Hong Kong people have not lost. Whoever preseveres till to end will win. Hong Kong people add oil!

2019年8月26日晚上9時許，25歲的郭先生（Kwok Chi Wai, Calvin，中文名字不詳）從觀塘順天邨天韻樓高處墮下，倒卧大廈天井，救護員送往聯合醫院後證實死亡。(Wikipedia)

At 9 o'clock on the evening of August 26, 2019, the 25-year-old Mr. Kwok (Kwok Chi Wai, Calvin, Chinese name is unknown) fell from the height of Tianyun Building in Shuntian Village, Kwun Tong, lying on the patio of the building. The medics sent him to the United Christian Hospital and confirmed his death.
