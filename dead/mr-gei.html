姬先生 Mr. Gei (16)

2019年9月2日下午4時46分，屯門友愛邨一名16歲姬姓少年於愛智樓高處墮下，當場身亡。LIHKG討論區同日曾有人以「各位好多謝大家，我諗我要走啦，再見」為主題開帖，惟未能證實是否死者所為，現時帖文已遭刪去。雖然網上有關資料較少，但網民一般都認定姬姓少年為另一位自殺「手足」。

9月2日開學後，網上流傳一張沙田官立中學通告，稱該校一名學生於9月2日不幸離世，惟未有列明原因。由於當時網民懷疑警方於8月31日在太子站的行動中「搞出人命」，所以此通告引起不少關注，及後終有人證實通告提及的學生為自殺身亡的姬姓少年。(EVCHK)

At 4:46 pm on September 2, 2019, a 16-year-old, Gei-surnamed youth from You Ai Village, Tuen Mun fell from the heights of Ai Chi House and died on the spot. Some people in the LIHKG forum posted a post on the same day with the theme:  "Thank you very much, I think I have to go, see you later". However, the post has not been confirmed. Although there is little relevant information on the Internet, Internet users generally believe that the Gei-surnamed juvenile is another "brother" who committed suicide. After the school started on September 2, a notice from Shatian Government Middle School was circulated online stating that a student of the school died unfortunately on September 2, but the reason was not specified. At that time, netizens suspected that the police "had taken lives" in the Prince Edward Station operation on August 31, so this notice attracted a lot of attention. Later, it was confirmed that the student mentioned in the notice was a teenager named Gei who committed suicide.
