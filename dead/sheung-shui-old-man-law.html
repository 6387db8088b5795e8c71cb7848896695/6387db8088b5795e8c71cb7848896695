上水羅老翁 Sheung Shui Old Man Law (70)

2019年11月13日中午約12時，在北區大會堂對開的龍運街。消息指，捆綁近30名操鄉音男女與黑人黑衣示威者，疑因清理路障的問題發生衝突，雙方拾起地上磚頭當武器互掟，混亂中一名70歲姓羅的男子被磚頭擊中頭部，當時老翁正在用電話拍攝。老翁即時倒地昏迷，之後送往北區醫院，再轉送至威爾斯醫院神經外科接受治療，情況危殆，14日10時51分證實不治，案件將會轉交死因裁判官跟進。調查科高級警司李桂華指。警察最先起訴案列為傷人案，後替代謀殺案。15日，市民自發有市民指，打死老人就是不對，何況對方並沒有打人。來到北區大會堂附近悼念羅老伯。(Wikipedia)

At about 12 o'clock on November 13, 2019, at Longyun Street, opposite the North District City Hall, the news pointed out that a group of nearly 30 people with country accents and a group of black-clad demonstrators were suspected to be in conflicts about clearing roadblocks. The two sides picked up the bricks on the ground as weapons, and a 70-year-old man named Luo was hit in the head while shooting the scene by phone. The old man fell into a coma immediately, was sent to the North District Hospital, and then, being critically ill, was transferred to the Department of Neurosurgery, Wells Hospital for treatment. At 10:51 on the 14th, he was confirmed dead; the case will be referred to the coroner follow up. Li Guihua, Senior Superintendent of the Investigation Division, said that the police had locked in suspected persons involved. He was the first civilian to be killed in a demonstration. The police first listed the case as a wounded case and later changed it to a murder case. On the 15th, the citizens spontaneously came to the North District Hall to mourn Luo Laobo. Some members of the public said that it is wrong to kill the elderly, not to mention that the other party has not beaten people.

Rest in peace
